Our Mission

To create a safer and more reliable public transportation ecosystem in Bangladesh by empowering commuters with real-time insights and community-driven feedback.

Our Vision

A future where every commuter in Bangladesh can travel safely and confidently, supported by transparent information and a connected community of fellow travelers.

Our Values

Transparency, safety, community collaboration, and continuous improvement drive everything we do at TrustTrack.

Our Journey

2024

The Idea

Recognizing the need for better transparency in public transport, our founders came together with a shared vision to create positive change.

Early 2025

Research & Development

Extensive market research and user interviews led to the development of TrustTrack's core features and user experience.

Mid 2025

Platform Launch

TrustTrack officially launched, providing Bangladeshi commuters with their first dedicated transport review platform.

Future

Expanding Impact

Plans to expand features, improve emergency response capabilities, and create partnerships with transport authorities.
