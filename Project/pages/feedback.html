Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to get a response?

We typically respond to all feedback within 24-48 hours during business days. High-priority issues are addressed even faster, often within a few hours.

Do I need to provide my contact information?

Contact information is optional. However, providing your email allows us to follow up with you about your feedback and notify you when issues are resolved.

What types of feedback do you accept?

We welcome all types of feedback including bug reports, feature requests, suggestions for improvement, general comments, and even appreciation. Every input helps us improve.

Is my feedback kept confidential?

Yes, all feedback is treated confidentially. We never share your personal information with third parties, and any public use of feedback is done anonymously.
